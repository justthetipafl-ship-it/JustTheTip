ℹ️ App Guide
How everything works — colour codes, ratings, data inputs, and workflow
🚀 Getting Started — Data Inputs
↑ Player CSV
Season averages for all players. Powers the Scanner, Snags, Elite, and Multi Builder. Load this first — it also provides positions for the DVP Game Log.
↑ Team Def
Last 10 games — what each team concedes. Powers all MU ratings and position-specific matchup panels in player modals.
↑ Team Form
Last 10 games — what each team produces. Powers Style Clash on fixture cards and the Teams page attack stats.
↑ DVP Game Log
Per-player game log (Champion Data format). Unlocks L5/L10 averages, hit rates, DVP% badges, prop calculator history, recent form table, and Multi Builder scoring. Most valuable data source.
Load via Bundle: Use the JTT Merger tool to combine all 4 CSVs into one JTT_Bundle.json, then click 📦 Load Bundle. All data loads at once in the correct order automatically.
🎯 Matchup (MU) Ratings
Compares what a team concedes vs the league average. Positive % = soft matchup. Uses Team Form data when loaded (recent form), otherwise season averages (Team Def).

🔍 Players Scanner
Table & Columns
Stats marked $ are bettable markets (disposals, goals, marks, tackles, clearances, kicks, handballs, behinds, fantasy). Click headers to sort.

Green cell = positive team MU · Blue cell + D = positive DVP
↑ = L5 avg 5%+ above season · ↓ = L5 below

Use the Columns panel in the sidebar to show/hide stats. Toggle groups on/off.
Sidebar Filters
Filter by team, position, opponent, min games, min TOG%. The ↑ Above pos avg only toggle shows only players performing above their position group average for the current sort stat.

Position Override — in the player modal, click the position tag to change a player's position. Affects DVP lookups, peer comparisons, and above-avg toggle. Persists across sessions.
🏟️ Teams Scanner
Sortable table of all 18 teams across 30+ stats — attack (For) and defence (Against). Click column headers to sort. Use the sidebar to toggle For only / Against only / Both. Colour coded green→red relative to the 18-team pool.

Click any team name to open the Team Modal — shows attack stats, opposition stats, DVP rankings by position, player list, and if a fixture is set, the current week's matchup panel.
📅 Fixture Page
Fixture Cards
Set up to 9 games for the round. Each card shows mini MU ratings for Disposals, Clearances, Goals, Marks, and Tackles.

Click 🔍 Focus This Game to filter the Scanner to only that game with opponent MU auto-applied.
Style Clash Verdict Tags
🔥 High-scoring — both sides above avg
📈 Above-avg output — mild positive lean
🛡️ Defensive contest — defences on top
🎯 Goals on — forwards expected to score
📊 Disposal game — high-disposal match
💪 Contested — contested possession battle
🏉 Snags & ⚡ Elite Pages
🏉 Snags — Forwards
Top-10 key forwards for goals, shots, marks on lead, marks inside 50, and forward 50 possessions. Hot Matchups (bettable stats) shown first.
⚡ Elite — All Positions
Top-10 players across every tracked stat alongside the Top 5 Teams Allowing — instantly see who the best players are and who their soft matchups are.
🛡️ DVP — Defence vs Position
Measures what each team concedes to each position group specifically — more precise than overall MU because a team might be soft on Midfielder disposals but tough on Key Forward goals.

Requires DVP Game Log. Select a position in the filter to see a full ranked table across 20 stats including DT, disposals, clearances, contested possessions, marks inside 50, intercepts, metres gained, score involvements and more. Click column headers to re-sort. Rank column shows #1 (easiest) to #18 (toughest).
👤 Player Modal
L5 / L10 / Season Toggle
Top right of the modal header. Switches all stat cards between Season averages (from player CSV) and Last 5 or Last 10 game averages (from DVP Game Log). Requires DVP Game Log. Players without log data auto-fall back to Season.
Prop Line Calculator
Select any stat (incl. clearances, inside 50s, cont poss, score inv) and enter a line. Shows hit rate, avg vs line, avg over/under, and a bar chart of every game.

📍 Amber bars = games against this week's opponent (requires fixture).
Games slider — restrict to last N games (L5, L10 etc).
Min TOG% slider — exclude games where player was managed.
Opponent Context
Position-specific breakdown of what the opponent concedes — different stats for mids, forwards, defenders, rucks. Shows % vs league avg with direction arrows. DVP pills appear below.
Recent Form & Similar Players
Recent Form — last 10 games, colour-coded vs season avg. L5 pinned row at bottom.

Similar Players vs Opp — last 10 games by same position players who have faced this opponent, sorted by recency. Shows when opponent is set.
🎯 Multi Builder
Auto-generates suggested multi legs for each fixture game. Analyses every player for Disposals, Goals, Marks, Tackles, Clearances, and Fantasy — scoring each leg on avg vs line gap, DVP, L5 form, consistency, hit rate, and position-specific matchup signals. Only positive matchup signals are shown on cards.
Leg Card Badges
L5 tag = trending up last 5 games.
✓ Consistent = low variance (CV < 0.35).
⚡ Variance = boom-bust player (CV > 0.7).
DVP +X% = positive position group matchup.
Context signals show only positive matchup factors relevant to that specific market and position.
Quality Gates
Legs must pass: score ≥ 3, estimated hit prob ≥ 55%, avg gap ≥ 0.3 above line. Legs with DVP < -10% or strongly negative MU environment are rejected. Max 9 legs per game. One leg per player enforced.
⚠️ Fair odds disclaimer: Probability shown assumes legs are independent. SGM legs within the same game are correlated — actual bookmaker odds will differ. Use as a directional guide only.
📝 Notes
Tag players, teams, games, or stats with notes. Add from the Notes tab, or via the 📝 button on any player row or fixture card. Notes are stored locally in your browser. Click any note to jump back to the relevant player, team, or game. Filter by type using the pill buttons at the top.

Just The Tip — AFL Prop Scanner
👤 Player Modal
Click any player row in the Scanner (or any player name link) to open a full breakdown modal. The modal has several sections stacked in order:
Stat Cards
Season averages across all tracked stats, grouped by category. Each card shows the value, % above/below position average, team MU rating, and DVP% where available.

Green card border = positive DVP matchup.
Faint green = positive team MU.
Prop Line Calculator
Select a stat and enter any line to instantly see: hit rate (hits/total), season avg vs line gap, avg when over, avg when under, and a game-by-game bar chart. Requires DVP Game Log for real hit rate data.
vs Opponent Context
When a fixture game is focused or an opponent is selected, shows a position-specific breakdown of what that opponent concedes — different stats for mids vs key forwards vs defenders vs rucks. Each stat shows the value, % vs league avg, and a ▲/▼ direction arrow. DVP pills for the player's position group appear below. Uses Team Form when available.
Recent Form & Similar Players
Recent Form — last 10 games from the DVP log as a mini table. Each stat is colour-coded green (above season avg) or red (below). An L5 avg pinned row shows how recent form compares to the full season.

Similar Players vs Opp — all players of the same position group who have faced this opponent in the game log, sorted by SC points.
🎯 Multi Builder
Auto-generates suggested multi legs for each fixture game. Analyses every player for Disposals, Goals, Marks, Tackles, and Fantasy lines — scoring each leg on avg-vs-line gap, matchup (MU), DVP, L5 form, consistency, and historical hit rate.
Leg Card Badges
Green avg badge = avg comfortably above the line (3+).
Yellow avg badge = avg above the line but close.
↑ L5 tag = player trending up in last 5 games.
✓ Consistent = low variance scorer (CV < 0.35).
⚡ Variance = boom-bust player (CV > 0.7).

Hit rate: 68% (14g) = real data from game log. ~65% est. = statistical estimate when no log loaded.
How Lines Are Set
Disposals: rounded avg minus 1 (minimum 1).
Goals: floor(avg) when it gives a cleaner gap, else round(avg). Must be ≥1.
Marks: round(avg) minus 1. Requires avg ≥ 3.
Tackles: round(avg) minus 1. Requires avg ≥ 4.
Fantasy: tiered to common bookmaker lines (55, 60, 65, 70, 75, 80, 85, 90, 100, 110, 120+).

Scoring weights L5 form at 65% and season avg at 35%. Only one leg per player is allowed (no correlated legs). Click legs to build a multi — the summary shows fair combined odds.
⚠️ Fair odds disclaimer: The summary panel calculates probability assuming legs are independent. SGM legs within the same game are correlated — actual bookmaker odds will differ. Use as a directional guide only.
